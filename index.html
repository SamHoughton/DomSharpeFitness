About Dom

I'VE BEEN WHERE YOU ARE.

I didn't always look or feel like this. My own transformation between 2022 and 2025 is the reason I'm so passionate about helping others.

Honestly, I didn't notice the change myself until I looked back at old photos. What made the difference wasn't some extreme diet or punishing programme. It was learning to enjoy my training, staying mindful (not strict) with food, and showing up consistently. I still drink. I still eat junk food, just in moderation most of the time.

I'm proud of what I've achieved, and even more passionate about helping others find that same balance and confidence. Based at Bannatyne Fairfield, I coach 1:1 across weight training, mobility, weight loss and accountability. Programmes built around your life, not the other way round.

Questions

GOT A QUESTION?

Not at all. Dom works with all levels, from complete beginners to experienced lifters. The programme is built around where you are right now, not where you think you should be.

All sessions are at Bannatyne Fairfield, Kingsley Ave, Fairfield Park, Hitchin SG5 4JJ. The gym has a full range of equipment for weight training, cardio and mobility work.

Sessions are typically 60 minutes. The first session is slightly longer as Dom will go through your goals, any injuries or limitations, and how you train currently.

Let Dom know before your first session. He programmes around injuries and works closely with clients who have mobility issues, back pain, or long-term conditions. If something is outside his scope he will tell you honestly.

Dom provides practical nutrition guidance as part of his coaching. Not a rigid meal plan, but realistic advice you can actually follow. He won't tell you to cut out everything you enjoy.

Most clients notice a difference in how they feel within a few weeks: more energy, better movement, stronger in the gym. Visible physical changes typically follow after 6 to 8 weeks of consistent training and nutrition. Everyone's different.
